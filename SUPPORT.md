# Support

> How to get help with RepoWrit.

---

## Getting Help

### Community Support (All Plans)

- **Bug reports** — [Open an issue](https://github.com/RepoWrit/repowrit/issues/new?template=bug_report.md)
- **Feature requests** — [Open an issue](https://github.com/RepoWrit/repowrit/issues/new?template=feature_request.md)
- **Documentation** — [repowrit.com/support](https://repowrit.com/support)

### Email Support

- **General & privacy:** [support@repowrit.com](mailto:support@repowrit.com)
- **Security:** [security@repowrit.com](mailto:security@repowrit.com)

We typically respond within 24 hours on business days for all plans. Paid plans receive priority handling per the table below.

---

## Response Times

| Priority | Hobbyist (Free) | BYOK ($4.99/mo) | Team ($20/seat/mo) | Enterprise ($49.99/seat/mo) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Critical** (service down, security) | Best effort | 12 hours | 4 hours | 1 hour |
| **High** (feature broken) | Best effort | 24 hours | 12 hours | 4 hours |
| **Medium** (degraded experience) | Best effort | 48 hours | 24 hours | 12 hours |
| **Low** (question, minor issue) | Best effort | 72 hours | 48 hours | 24 hours |

*Response times are measured during business hours (Mon–Fri, 9 AM – 6 PM IST). Critical issues for Team and Enterprise customers are monitored 7 days/week.*

### Priority Definitions

- **Critical** — RepoWrit is completely unavailable, or a security vulnerability has been reported. No workaround exists.
- **High** — A core feature (documentation generation, executive briefings, architecture maps, Ask RepoWrit) is not functioning. A workaround may exist.
- **Medium** — A feature is working but with degraded performance or incorrect output. The issue is reproducible.
- **Low** — A question about functionality, a minor UI issue, or a feature request.

---

## What to Include in a Support Request

To help us resolve your issue quickly, please include:

1. **Your GitHub username** — So we can look up your account.
2. **Repository name and branch** — The affected repository and branch (if applicable).
3. **Description** — What you expected vs. what happened.
4. **Steps to reproduce** — How we can see the same issue.
5. **Screenshots or logs** — Any relevant error messages or unexpected output.
6. **Plan tier** — Hobbyist / BYOK / Team / Enterprise.

---

## Service Status

If RepoWrit is experiencing downtime or degraded performance, we will post updates on our GitHub organization page and notify affected customers by email.

## Billing Support

RepoWrit uses [Lemon Squeezy](https://lemonsqueezy.com) as our Merchant of Record. For billing questions:

- **Manage your subscription** — Visit your [account page](https://repowrit.com/account) to view plan details, update payment method, change seats (Team / Enterprise), or cancel.
- **Refunds** — All paid plans include a **5-day money-back guarantee**. Email [support@repowrit.com](mailto:support@repowrit.com) within 5 days of a charge for a full refund — no questions asked. See the full [Refund Policy](https://repowrit.com/refund).
- **Invoices** — Available through the Lemon Squeezy customer portal, accessible from your account page.

---

## Cancellation

You can cancel your subscription at any time from your [account page](https://repowrit.com/account). When you cancel:

- Your plan remains active until the end of the current billing period.
- After the billing period ends, your account reverts to the Hobbyist (free) tier.
- AI-generated documentation, briefings, and architecture maps remain accessible.
- You can re-subscribe at any time.

To delete your account entirely, email [support@repowrit.com](mailto:support@repowrit.com).
